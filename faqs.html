Frequently Asked Questions

What are the gym's opening hours?
Our gym is open 24/7, allowing you to work out whenever it suits you best!
Do you offer personal training sessions?
Yes, we have experienced personal trainers available for one-on-one coaching sessions tailored to your fitness goals.
What equipment is available at the gym?
We have a wide range of equipment, including cardio machines, free weights, resistance machines, and functional training equipment to suit all your workout needs.
Are there any nutrition counseling services available?
Yes, we offer nutrition counseling services with certified nutritionists to help you achieve your health and fitness goals through personalized meal plans and advice.
Do you offer childcare services?
Yes, we have a dedicated kids' area and provide childcare services during peak hours to help parents focus on their workouts.
What membership plans do you offer?
We offer monthly, quarterly, and annual memberships. Discounts are available for long-term plans.
Is there a free trial available for new members?
Absolutely! We offer a one-week free trial for new members to explore our facilities and services before committing to a membership.
Are group classes included in my membership?
Yes! We offer yoga, HIIT, strength training, and other group classes at no extra cost.
Do you have locker rooms and showers?
Absolutely! We provide clean and spacious locker rooms with shower facilities.
What safety measures are in place at the gym?
We follow strict cleaning protocols, provide sanitizing stations, and ensure that equipment is regularly disinfected. Additionally, our staff is trained in first aid and emergency procedures.
Can I freeze or cancel my membership?
Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months or cancel with a 30-day notice.
